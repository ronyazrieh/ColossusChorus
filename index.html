To all adventurers, a challenge has been posed with a promise of ancient wisdom as its prize. Join together for this 4-player, local multiplayer adventure and face the trials of the mythical wizard, Merrill the Omniscient. With every delve, explore a newly-generated dungeon, battle against all kinds of enemies, and collect legendary loot. Along the way, relive the past experiences of the wizard in competitive and collaborative minigames.

Updates

We’ve rolled out the latest patch to refine combat balance, optimize performance, and enrich narrative depth. In this update, the wizard’s spellbook now includes two new rituals that reward team coordination; enemy AI has been improved to smartly adapt to player positioning so that each dungeon dive feels more dynamic. We’ve also expanded the loot system with rare artifacts that grant unique ability modifiers, allowing players to customize their builds in meaningful ways.

On the quality side, we fixed dozens of reported issues, including stuttering in the Forest of Whispers, audio dropouts during the Sound Temple, and incorrect collision behavior on the final boss platform. We also polished the UI by adding clearer tooltips and accessibility improvements, such as keyboard navigation support in inventory and improved text contrast for readability.

Looking ahead, the next update will introduce the first seasonal event and a new multiplayer mode that emphasizes cooperative strategy and shared objectives. Follow our channel and check the dev blog for deeper behind-the-scenes dev logs, so you never miss a beat as The Colossus Chorus evolves.
